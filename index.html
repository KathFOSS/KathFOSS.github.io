Kathford FOSS Community

We are a team of students who believe in the usages of Free/Open source software(FOSS). The primary objective of our community is to promote, develop and diversify the usages of Free/Open Source software for self motivated and enthusiastic students by organizing various workshops/Programs, seminar, competitions on related topics. We believe in freedom.
