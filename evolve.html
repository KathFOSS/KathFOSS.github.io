Kathford FOSS Community (KATHFOSS) of Kathford Int’l college of Engineering and Management is going to organize a code camp - “Evolve ( ) 2.0” at Kathford Int’l College of Engineering and Management in the support of Kathford Robotics Club (ROBOKATH). Evolve( ) 2.0 is a series of Evolve ( ) organized by KATHFOSS each year. Our first code camp i.e., “Evolve( ) 1.0” was organized in May, 2015 and due to some internal problem we couldn’t give it a continuity in 2016 and thus we came up with Evolve( ) 2.0 in 2017. Here we promote the ideas of the students and help them code their ideas and change them into reality by providing them with mentors, a suitable environment and many more. Students will need to form a group of minimum 2 and maximum 4 members and then submit their ideas among which top 10 ideas will be selected for “Evolve( ) 2.0”. The selected 10 teams will be provided with mentors to code their idea and they will have to pitch their idea and present a demo as well. Based on those demo and pitch, winners will be selected and awarded accordingly. This code camp will be very helpful for each and every individual to improve his/her personal skills to enhance the practical knowlegde which helps him/her in different stages of career development.

Objectives of Evolve ( )
  1. To help students sharpen their minds and give them an opportunity to develop their practical knowledge.
  2. To provide platform for interaction and healthy competition among the creative minds in technical fields and marketing managaement.
  3. To provide exposure to the young students.
  4. To develop team work among the students.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

FAQ will be available soon

  • Who can participate?

    Any interested group of students with an idea can participate in Evolve() 2.0.

  • Do we need to pay the registration fee?

    No, it's completely free to participate.

  • What kind of projects do we need to submit and what kind of projects will be prioritized?

    You can submit any kind of project (web application, android application, desktop application, etc). Projects will be prioritized on the basis of following:

    • Impact: What kind of problem does it solves?
    • Creativity: How creative is the approach?
    • Product: How well does this project fit the needs of the challange it addresses? How user friendly is the technology?
    • Sustainibility: How good is the plan for next steps?
    • Presentation: How well is the story of the project presented? Why it is importat?

  • Can we apply with our already completed projects?

    Yes. You can, but only if you haven't presented it anywhere else.

  • Which programming language do we need to choose?

    It completely depends on you. You can complete the project in any language as per your ease.

  • Can I involve in more than one group?

    No, you cannot be a member of more than one team.

  • I’m a single applicant. Can I participate?

    No, you must form a group of 2-4 members.

  • Do we need to submit a project proposal?

    Yes, after being selected in Evovle() 2.0, you will have to submit a project proposal.

  • How can we know if our project gets selected?

    The team leader will receive a mail after selection process is completed.

  • Who will be speaker in motivational seminar on entrepreneurship?

    He/She can be anyone from related field. Each team will be notified about the speakers.

  • What do we need to present in short intial pitch during PHASE III?

    You will have to present about your idea(What does it do and how?) that you have submitted.

  • Who will be our mentors?

    Each team will be assigned a mentor as per their project (i.e., a team working in python will be assigned a mentor who has a good knowledge of python).

  • If our project is large and cannot be fully completed within the final demonstration phase, what can we do?

    You must be able to submit a working prototype of your project.

  • Who will be Judges during final demo?

    A panel of judges of respective fields will be judging your project during selection as well as during the final demo.

  • What will be the prize for winners?

    Winners will be awarded with a trophy and a surprise. The surprise will be disclosed in final phase during the final demo. It will be worth winning for sure.
